SoHo Experience Guide Now Live

Your resource for understanding and implementing the most current SoHo product experience.

The Infor SoHo Experience Guide is now live, complete with the most comprehensive collection of specifications and HTML controls we've ever published. As your single, most important resource for "creating experiences people love", this guide explains not just the what about a control (detailed styling specifications), but also the how and why (usage guidelines).

In this guide you'll find:

  • Information about the SoHo design vision
  • Tips on what kinds of interactions are the best fit for your application
  • Overview topics that provide insight into user interactions
  • Working examples and the code needed to implement the HTML controls
  • Spec diagrams to illustrate the composition of various controls
