Circle Pie

Purpose Pie charts are used to compare parts of a whole. The pieces of the graph are proportional to the fraction of the whole in each category; each slice of the pie is relative to the size of that category in the group as a whole. Pie charts give you a snapshot of how a group is broken down into smaller pieces.
Note: Unlike bar and line charts, pie charts can only show a single series of variables. Pie charts can not be contained in 3x size. It will be only provided in 1x and 2x.
